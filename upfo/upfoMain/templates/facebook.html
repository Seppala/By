The Byy

The easiest way to meet your friends more often

Have you ever wanted to do something but you didn't know who to call?

We think it's super important that we meet our friends often, and even meet friends we haven't seen for a while.

That's what we're doing with Byy. It's the easiest way to meet your friends more often and even meet friends you haven't met for a while.

How does it work?

You just log in with Facebook and click the "I'm up for something"-button. You'll see the friends that are up for something and they'll see you.

Byy is optimized for mobile, but you can use it in your browser as well.
