What is By?

Basically By is just a button that let's your friends know that you're up for something. You just log in with Facebook and click the "I'm up for something"-button. You'll see a list of friends that are up for something and they'll see you. Then all you have to do is call them up or go knock on their door (old school)!

What's the use?

When you go out, check By and see who else is up for something, gather more people and meet more friends.If ever you find yourself in a situation where you'd like to do something but you don't know who to call, just check By.

By? Epic?

'By' means 'Village' in Swedish. Back when people lived in smaller villages you would meet people you know all the time. We want to make it more like that in the cities we live in now. That's why we created By.
