By? Epic?

'By' means 'Village' in Swedish. Back when people lived in smaller villages you would meet people you know all the time. We want to make it more like that in the cities we live in now. That's why we created By.

Who's up for something?

Basically By is just a button from which you can tell your friends that you're up for something. You just log in with Facebook and click the "I'm up for something"-button. You'll see the friends that are up for something and they'll see you. Then all you have to do is call them up!

Why?

Ever had a situation where you'd like to do something but you didn't know who to call? Just get used to using By and get your friends on it and you will not have that problem again.
