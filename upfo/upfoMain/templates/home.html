Meet more friends

What's better than meeting more friends? We think one of the problems that the large cities we live in today is that it's not easy enough to bump into friends. We want to change that. That's why we create By. It's the easiest way to tell your friends your up for something.

By?

By means village in Swedish. Back when most of us lived is smaller villages, it was easy to connect with people. You pretty much knew everyone. We want to bring some of that magic back.

What does By do?

Basically By's just one button - The "I'm up for something button". You click the button and your Facebook friends who are also on By can immediately see that you're up for something. So check out who's up for something and give them a call. It's very early days for By, we're developing it every day.
